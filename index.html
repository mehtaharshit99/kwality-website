Our Story

Delhi's Most Cherished Table

Nestled in the heart of Connaught Place since the mid-20th century, Kwality has fed generations of Delhiites and visitors alike. From the fragrant curries of North India to the smoky grandeur of our BBQ, every dish here carries the weight of tradition.

We bring you time-honoured Mughlai recipes alongside inventive contemporary plates — all rooted in the same uncompromising belief in quality ingredients and bold, honest flavour.
